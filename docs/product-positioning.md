# Product Positioning

## Core Split

The LINE product and the Coaching Company product should not be treated as the same service tier.

They serve different purposes.

## LINE Position

LINE is best positioned as:

- entry product
- habit-building product
- lightweight daily practice
- low-friction onboarding to the broader ecosystem

Its role is to help learners start, continue, and build consistency.

This makes it suitable for:

- free or lower-cost access
- daily one-question practice
- simple routine reinforcement
- audience building and conversion into higher-value offerings

## Coaching Company Position

The Coaching Company should be positioned as the serious, higher-commitment offering.

Its role is to:

- diagnose current level
- create a roadmap
- manage study strategy
- run session-based practice
- track current state
- intervene when weakness patterns appear
- help the learner actually pass

In short:

- LINE helps people start and continue
- Coaching Company helps people pass

## Shared Problem Bank, Different Orchestration

The question bank may be shared.

However, the delivery logic should not be the same.

That means:

- the underlying questions can come from the same source
- the sequencing, purpose, and reuse rules should differ by product

## Why Duplicate Questions Are A Problem

If the same learner uses both LINE and Coaching Company, uncontrolled duplication will feel lazy and lower trust.

A learner in the premium experience should not feel like they are just seeing the same sequence again.

## Recommended Rule

Questions can be shared across products, but question exposure should be tracked across channels.

The system should distinguish:

- the question itself
- where it was shown
- why it was shown

Examples of purpose:

- daily practice
- onboarding assessment
- review
- weakness check
- confidence rebuild

The same question may be reused only when the reason is clear.

## Business Framing

This creates a natural value ladder:

- LINE
  low-friction entry and habit
- Coaching Company
  structured premium support and exam outcome focus
