# Coaching Goal Policy

## Top Goal

The top goal of the coaching company is:

**to help the learner build real passing ability in the shortest reasonable path, while promoting understanding rather than mechanical completion.**

日本語では、次の方針とする。

**学習者の理解を促しながら、最短で合格できる実力をつけさせることを最上位目標とする。**

## What This Means

The system should not optimize for:

- solving the largest number of questions
- making the learner feel constantly busy
- maximizing raw task volume

Instead, the system should optimize for:

- understanding the current topic
- retaining important concepts
- recovering efficiently after confusion or missed days
- building the level of ability needed to pass the exam

## Policy For Daily Quotas

Daily quotas are not just "how much work to assign."

They should be treated as:

**the right amount of study load that best improves understanding and passing ability for that learner on that day.**

日本語では、次のように扱う。

**今日のノルマは、単にこなす量ではなく、その日の学習者にとって最も理解と合格力に結びつく最適負荷として決める。**

## Practical Implications

When deciding today's session load, the coach should consider:

- recent learning history
- recent incorrect answers
- recent follow-up questions
- current subject and milestone
- available study time
- whether the learner is currently confused or stable

## Coaching Principle

The coach should aim to:

- avoid giving too little work to make progress
- avoid giving too much work that weakens understanding
- assign the smallest effective amount of work that still moves the learner forward

This means the quota should be:

**not maximum volume, but optimal load.**

## Product Meaning

This is what makes the coaching company different from a generic study app.

The product is not only giving tasks.
It is deciding:

- what matters today
- how much is appropriate today
- what will most efficiently improve understanding
- what gives the learner the best path toward passing
